This is the first post. I've been meaning to do this for a while — a place to
think in public about the things I find myself returning to.

The working theme is **decision making under uncertainty**. That bleeds into
value investing, probabilistic thinking, risk, and the gap between a good
decision and a good outcome. Those are different things, and most of the
interesting ideas live in that gap.

## What to expect

Short notes, not essays. If something is worth a longer piece I'll write it.
Most of the time I want to publish an idea before it calcifies into certainty.

## Format

Markdown files, plain and portable. Everything here is designed to survive a
platform change — because, empirically, every platform eventually changes.
